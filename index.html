Biography

Born in 1994, Jacob Nan has lived an adventurous life both in and out of the virtual world.

After graduating highschool in 2012, he had the opportunity to travel the world. Italy, France, Great Britain, and Canada are a few of the wonderful places his adventures have taken him to. Excelling in Math as well as Speech and Debate, Jacob has won multiple awards for his talents during his educational career.

Jacob Nan is also a father to a beautiful 3 month-old baby by the name of Abigail. Jacob's hobbies include Cooking, coding, and PC Gaming, when he's not too busy changing diapers of course.
